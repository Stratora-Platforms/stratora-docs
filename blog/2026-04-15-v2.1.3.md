Stratora v2.1.3 is a feature release focused on audit logging, escalation team improvements, and contact management. All components (Server, Agent, Collector) are now versioned in lockstep at 2.1.3.

<!-- truncate -->

### Comprehensive Audit Logging

Audit log coverage has been significantly expanded. Full CRUD events are now captured for collectors, site photos, contact bulk imports, OIDC provider configuration, component enrollment, server startup, and migration application. Self-service password changes, session cleanup, and IdP contact sync operations (create, update, merge) are all recorded with proper actor attribution.

A new DB-level immutability trigger (migration 146) prevents direct mutation of audit log rows, ensuring the audit trail cannot be tampered with after the fact. Retention is managed exclusively through a SECURITY DEFINER function.

### Escalation Team Enhancements

Escalation teams have received a significant overhaul:

- **Contact junction table** — contact references are now stored in a dedicated `escalation_channel_contacts` junction table (migration 144), replacing the previous inline array. Email, Slack, and Teams dispatch resolves contacts from this junction.
- **Live on-call preview** — a banner in the rotation schedule shows the current position holder and next shift handoff time.
- **Drag-and-drop reordering** for rotation members, with keyboard-accessible fallback controls.
- **Save-time warnings** when rotation members lack phone or email for targeted notification channels.
- **SMS/Voice targeting** — On-Call #1, All Members, and Specific Contacts chips are now fully honored by backend dispatch logic, with explanatory tooltips on each option.
- **Dispatch audit entries** when SMS/Voice notifications are skipped due to missing phone numbers.

### Contact Registry Improvements

- **IdP sync merge** — existing manual contacts are promoted to managed in place when an email match is found at login, with an audit merge entry recorded.
- **Two-step modal redesign** — creating or editing a contact now uses a clean Basic Info / Notification Channels flow instead of expandable "advanced options."
- **Phone number normalization** via react-phone-number-input enforces country codes and auto-formatting across all phone inputs.
- **ContactPicker filtering** — the picker now only shows contacts that have the required channel configured (email, phone, Slack, or Teams as appropriate).

### Bug Fixes

- User menu dropdown no longer clipped by TopNavBar overflow.
- IdP contact creation no longer produces NULL email on first sync.
- Alert toast regressions fixed: acknowledged alerts no longer re-appear, dismissing via "View" works correctly, tab sleep/wake no longer causes burst toasts, and toasts no longer render above modals.
- Escalation team clone now preserves junction-backed contact references.
- Escalation team delete of in-use teams now succeeds (FK cascade was already correct).
