We're excited to introduce **Reports**, a new top-level feature in Stratora that lets you turn your infrastructure monitoring data into polished PDF documents ready for clients, management, or compliance records.

## What's Included

**Site Health Reports** cover the metrics that matter most:

- **Health Score** — time-averaged percentage of healthy nodes across your sites
- **Uptime & Healthy Time** — availability tracking at the site level
- **Incident Analysis** — offline events with flap debounce and MTTR calculations
- **Health Heatmap** — color-coded visual timeline of site status
- **Trend Analysis** — are things getting better or worse?
- **Cross-Site Comparison** — ranked reliability view across all locations

## Built-in Templates

Three templates are included out of the box:

- **Site Health Summary** — 30-day overview (monthly reporting)
- **Weekly Site Health** — 7-day digest (weekly check-ins)
- **Quarterly Site Health Review** — 90-day executive summary (QBRs and client presentations)

## Custom Templates

Create your own templates with full control over:

- Which sites to include (all or specific)
- Time range (7 days, 30 days, 90 days, or 1 year)
- Which report sections to include

## Key Design Decisions

- **Maintenance-aware** — nodes in maintenance windows are excluded from health score calculations, so planned work doesn't distort your data
- **Flap debounce** — a 2-hour window prevents transient connectivity blips from inflating incident counts
- **Hourly snapshots** — reports use pre-aggregated data, so generating a report adds no load to your collectors

## Getting Started

Head to **Reports** in the sidebar to explore the built-in templates or create your own. Check out the [Reports documentation](/docs/reports) for full details.

## What's Next

Additional report types — including Capacity Planning, Alert Summary, and SLA Compliance — are planned for future releases.
